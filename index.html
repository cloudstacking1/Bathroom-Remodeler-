What is Mosaic Tile Installation?

Mosaic Tile Installation is the process of arranging and securing small tiles, often made from glass, ceramic, porcelain, or natural stone, to create decorative patterns or intricate designs on surfaces such as floors, walls, backsplashes, and shower areas. These tiles come in pre-mounted sheets or individual pieces, allowing for customizable and artistic layouts.
